# v0.8 Social and Sharing

## Goal

Let users share useful recipes and cooking wins without turning the app into a noisy social network.

## Status

Planned

## User outcomes

- Users can share recipes through native share sheets.
- Users can export readable recipe cards.
- Households can share grocery lists or meal plans.

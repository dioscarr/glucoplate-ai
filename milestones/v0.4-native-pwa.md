# v0.4 Native PWA

## Goal

Make GlucoPlate feel like a polished iOS and mobile web app while keeping it deployable as a PWA.

## Status

In progress

## User outcomes

- The app feels natural on iPhone Home Screen.
- Cook Mode stays readable and comfortable.
- Users can share recipes through the native share sheet.
- Notifications can deep-link back into useful screens.
- Saved recipes remain available offline.

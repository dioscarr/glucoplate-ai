Make GlucoPlate smarter over time by learning user preferences, constraints, and cooking habits.

## Status

- The app remembers favorite cuisines, dislikes, allergies, cooking skill, and family size.
- Suggestions improve based on saved and cooked recipes.

## Flavor Memory foundation

Authenticated, enterprise-scoped users can now record recipe interactions for the active household profile:

- `saved`
- `cooked`
- `dismissed`
- `repeated`

The API exposes interaction history and a compact profile summary. This creates a stable event contract before recommendation scoring is added, and keeps household members' taste signals isolated from one another.

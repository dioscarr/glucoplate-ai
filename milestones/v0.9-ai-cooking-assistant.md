# v0.9 AI Cooking Assistant

## Goal

Turn GlucoPlate into an active cooking companion that helps while users cook, not only before they start.

## Status

Planned

## User outcomes

- Users can ask cooking questions mid-recipe.
- Cook Mode adapts when something changes.
- The assistant can suggest substitutions, fix mistakes, and explain techniques.

# v0.7 Meal Planning

## Goal

Expand GlucoPlate from single-recipe generation into weekly planning and grocery preparation.

## Status

Planned

## User outcomes

- Users can generate a practical meal plan.
- Recipes convert into grocery lists.
- Plans account for preferences, time, and family size.
- Shopping becomes easier.
